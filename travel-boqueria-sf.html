📋 Route Summary

FromMercat de la Boqueria (Liceu)
ToSagrada Família (C/ Marina entrance)
MetroL2 Liceu → Sagrada Família (5 stops)
Duration~15 min on metro + 3 min walk
TicketT-Casual (€11.35 for 10 rides)

🚇 Step by Step

1

Exit Boqueria onto La Rambla

Walk 2 minutes to Liceu metro station (L2, green line). Entrance is on La Rambla itself.

2

Liceu → Sagrada Família

Take L2 (purple line) direction Badalona Pompeu Fabra. Ride 5 stops: Liceu → Sant Antoni → Passeig de Gràcia → Tetuan → Monumental → Sagrada Família.

3

Walk to C/ Marina entrance

Exit the station and walk 3 minutes to the Carrer de Marina entrance (the side entrance, not the main Nativity facade). You need to be there by 12:50 for your 13:00 timed entry.
