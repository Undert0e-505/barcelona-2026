💡 Fun Facts

🛫

Barcelona's airport is called Josep Tarradellas El Prat, named after a Catalan politician. Locals just call it "El Prat" — which also means "meadow" in Catalan.

⏰

Spain is on CEST (Central European Summer Time) — one hour ahead of the UK. You gain an hour on the way back!

✈️

easyJet was founded in 1995 and originally operated with planes leased from Air Foyle. Their first flight was from Luton to Glasgow for just £29.
