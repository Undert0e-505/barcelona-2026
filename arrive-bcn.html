💡 Fun Facts

🚌

The Aerobús is genuinely excellent — cheap, frequent, and has two useful stops. Going to the flat? Get off at Plaça Espanya and take L5 to Entença. Heading to La Boqueria? Stay on to Plaça Catalunya and walk 5 min down La Rambla.

🏛️

From Plaça Catalunya you're walking distance from the Gothic Quarter, Las Ramblas, and the cathedral. The whole centre is surprisingly compact.

☕

Grab a café amb llet and a pastry at any corner bar. The proper way to start a Barcelona morning.

🚇

Barcelona's metro has 12 lines. A T-Casual card (€11.35 for 10 rides) is the way to go.
