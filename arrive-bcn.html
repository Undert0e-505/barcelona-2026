💡 Fun Facts

🚌

The Aerobús is Barcelona's airport shuttle and it's genuinely excellent — cheap, frequent, and drops you right at Plaça Catalunya, the heart of the city. No need to mess about with taxis.

🏛️

From Plaça Catalunya you're walking distance from the Gothic Quarter, Las Ramblas, and the cathedral. The whole city centre is surprisingly compact.

☕

Once you arrive, grab a café amb llet (Catalan coffee with milk) and a pastry at any corner bar. It's the proper way to start a Barcelona morning.

🚇

Barcelona's metro has 12 lines and is one of the most efficient in Europe. A T-Casual card (€11.35 for 10 rides) is the way to go.
