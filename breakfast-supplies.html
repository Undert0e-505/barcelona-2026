🥐 Bakeries

🥐

Forn Mistet

Best croissants in the neighbourhood — the cruasans de xocolata are worth the walk. Tiny place, always a weekend queue.

Carrer de Còrsega, 229 · ~3 min walk
🥖

Forn Millet

Traditional Catalan bakery right on Provença — ensaimades, coca, fresh bread, and cheap coffee at the counter. Open early (07:00).

Carrer de Provença, 281 · ~2 min walk
🍞

Reial Forn Tecla

Proper neighbourhood bakery on Sardenya, one block from the flat. Fresh baguettes, entrepans, and pastries. Open from 07:00. Grab-and-go.

Carrer de Sardenya, 79 · ~1 min walk

☕ Cafés

☕

Cafè de l'Eixample

Traditional Catalan café. Cheap coffee and entrepans at the counter. No English menu, no pretension. You'll be the only tourists there. Open from 06:30.

Carrer de Mallorca, 378 · ~2 min walk
🥤

Federal Café

Aussie-style brunch — avocado toast, eggs benedict, flat whites. Outdoor terrace for sunny mornings. Slightly pricier but solid. Opens 09:00.

Carrer de Còrsega, 227 · ~3 min walk
☕

El Cafè de la Roca

Tiny neighbourhood café on Sardenya. Fast coffee, pastries, sandwiches. Locals-only vibe, cheap prices. Good for a quick caffeine hit on the way out.

Carrer de Sardenya, 98 · ~1 min walk

🛒 Supermarkets & Convenience

🛒

Condis Express

On your street — literally around the corner from the flat. Everything you need: milk, bread, cheese, ham, drinks, snacks, toiletries. Open 08:00–22:00 daily. This is your go-to.

Carrer de Rosselló, 184 · ~1 min walk · Daily 08:00–22:00
🏪

SuperCOR

Convenience store with longer hours than Condis. Slightly more expensive but open till 01:00. Good for late-night beer runs or forgotten supplies.

Carrer de Provença, 279 · ~2 min walk · Daily 08:00–01:00
🍎

Mercat de la Concepció

Covered market — fruit, veg, meat, fish, cheese, bread, jamón by the 100g. More expensive than supermarkets but the quality is outstanding. Great for picnic supplies and fresh fruit.

Carrer d'Aragó, 313 · ~3 min walk · Mon–Sat 08:00–20:30

🍷 Wine, Beer & Cava

🍾

Cava at Condis or SuperCOR

Spanish sparkling wine is absurdly cheap — Freixenet and Codorníu from €3–5 a bottle. Same quality as prosecco at half the price. Just buy it at Condis and drink it on the terrace.

Available at Condis (1 min) and SuperCOR (2 min)
🍷

Vila Viniteca

One of Barcelona's best wine shops. Catalan and Spanish wines from €4, staff who know everything. Takeaway bottles or order by the case. A few blocks away but worth it for a decent bottle.

Carrer de Provença · ~5 min walk
🍺

Beer at Condis / SuperCOR

Estrella Damm from €0.60/can, Moritz from €0.80. Cans go in the fridge at the flat. No need for a specialist shop — the supermarkets have you covered.

Available at Condis (1 min) and SuperCOR (2 min)
