🥐 Bakeries

🥐

Forn Mistet · 🗺️ Walk

The best croissants in the neighbourhood. Tiny bakery on Carrer de Còrsega, always a queue on weekends. Their cruasans de xocolata are worth the 4-minute walk.

Carrer de Còrsega, 229 · 3 min Mon–Sat
🍞

Baluard Forn de Pa · 🗺️ Walk

Artisan bakery with sourdough, focaccia, and traditional Catalan pastries. The coca de recapte (flatbread with roasted veg) is perfect for a quick breakfast on the go.

Carrer de Bilbao, 146 · 6 min Mon–Sat
🥖

L'Eclair Bakery · 🗺️ Walk

Sourdough specialist with excellent baguettes and pastries. Also does great sandwiches if you want something more substantial. The brot de pa (bread loaf) is outstanding.

Carrer de Còrsega, 283 · 5 min Tue–Sun
🧁

Forn Berta · 🗺️ Walk

Neighbourhood bakery on Carrer de Sant Joan. Solid croissants, ensaimades, and coffee. Open early (07:00) — good for a quick grab before heading out.

Carrer de Sant Joan, 30 · 5 min Mon–Sat 07:00

☕ Cafés (Quick Breakfast)

☕

Només Café · 🗺️ Walk

Specialty coffee on Rambla del Poblenou — also the finish point of the Poblenou street art walk. Excellent flat whites and pastries. 10-minute walk but worth it if you're heading that direction.

Rambla del Poblenou, 34 · 10 min Mon–Sun 08:00
🥤

Federal Café · 🗺️ Walk

Australian-style café on the same block as Forn Mistet. Avocado toast, eggs benedict, proper flat whites. Slightly pricier but the breakfast menu is solid. Outdoor terrace for sunny mornings.

Carrer de Còrsega, 227 · 3 min Mon–Sun 09:00
🥐

Cafè de l'Eixample · 🗺️ Walk

Traditional Catalan café on Carrer de Mallorca. Cheap coffee and entrepans (baguette sandwiches). No English menu, no pretension, just fast breakfast at the counter. You'll be the only tourists there.

Carrer de Mallorca, 378 · 2 min Mon–Sat 06:30

🛒 Supermarkets & Convenience

🛒

Condis Express · 🗺️ Walk

The nearest supermarket — literally on your street. Open 08:00–22:00 every day. Stocks everything: milk, bread, cheese, ham, drinks, snacks, toiletries. This is your go-to for supply runs.

Carrer de Rosselló, 184 · 1 min Daily 08:00–22:00
🛒

Condis (Larger) · 🗺️ Walk

Bigger branch with a wider selection — fresh meat counter, deli, more wine options. Worth the extra 2 minutes if you need specific items.

Carrer de Rosselló, 466 · 4 min Daily 08:00–22:00
🏪

SuperCOR · 🗺️ Walk

Convenience store with longer hours. Good for late-night beer, snacks, or anything you forgot. Slightly more expensive than Condis but open later.

Carrer de Provença, 279 · 5 min Daily 08:00–01:00
🍎

Mercat de la Concepció · 🗺️ Walk

Full covered market — fruit, veg, meat, fish, cheese, bread. More expensive than supermarkets but the quality is outstanding. Great for picnic supplies, fresh fruit, and jamón ibérico by the 100g.

Carrer d'Aragó, 313 · 6 min Mon–Sat 08:00–20:30

🍷 Wine, Beer & Alcohol

🍷

L'Oncle Suri · 🗺️ Walk

Natural wine bar and shop. Bottles from €8, excellent Catalan and Spanish selections. The staff know their stuff — tell them what you like and they'll recommend. Takeaway available.

Carrer de Còrsega, 261 · 4 min Tue–Sat 18:00–00:00
🍺

Condis / SuperCOR

Both supermarkets have a solid wine and beer section. Catalan wines from €3–5, Estrella Damm from €0.60/can. Cheapest option for stocking the flat.

See above for locations and hours
🍾

Cava (in supermarkets)

Spanish sparkling wine (Cava) is absurdly cheap in supermarkets — Freixenet and Codorníu from €3–5 a bottle. It's the same quality as prosecco at half the price. Buy it at Condis and drink it on the terrace.

Available at Condis, SuperCOR, and Mercat de la Concepció
