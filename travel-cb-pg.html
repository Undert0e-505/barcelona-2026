🚇 Step by Step

1

Exit Casa Batlló, walk to Passeig de Gràcia metro

Casa Batlló is on Passeig de Gràcia itself. The metro entrance is right there — look for the L3 green line entrance on the same street.

2

L3 Passeig de Gràcia → Vallcarca

Take L3 (green line) direction Canyelles. Ride 4 stops: Passeig de Gràcia → Diagonal → Lesseps → Vallcarca. Exit at Vallcarca.

3

Walk uphill to Park Güell

From Vallcarca station, it's a 15-minute uphill walk. Follow Carrer de Larrard — there are escalators partway up! You'll see signs for Park Güell. The entrance is on Carrer d'Olot.

4

Arrive by 12:00

Your timed entry is at 12:00 and valid for 30 min. Don't be late — leave Casa Batlló by 11:00 to arrive comfortably.

💡 Tips

🪜

Escalators on the hill

Barcelona installed outdoor escalators on Carrer de Larrard to help with the climb. They make the uphill walk much easier — look for them on the right side as you go up.

🕐

Alternative: Lesseps station

You could also get off at Lesseps (3 stops instead of 4), but the walk is longer (~20 min) and steeper. Vallcarca is the better choice — shorter and has the escalators.
