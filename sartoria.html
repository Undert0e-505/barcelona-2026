💡 Fun Facts

🍕

Sartoria Panatieri's motto is "From Farm to Pizza" — they source ingredients directly from local Catalan farms. The dough ferments for 72 hours.

🏆

They were ranked #20 on the 50 Top Pizza Europe 2024 list. Not bad for a place that also does takeaway.

🥂

The Eixample location on Carrer de Còrsega is the original. There's also a Gràcia branch, but you want this one — it's 10 minutes from your flat.

🇮🇹

The name is a mashup: "Sartoria" means tailor's workshop in Italian, and "Panatieri" is the Catalan spelling of Panatieri — the family behind it. So: a tailor-made pizza, essentially.
