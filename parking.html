🚗 Arrival & Exit

🎟️

On arrival: Drive up to the entry barrier and press for a ticket. Take your ticket to raise the barrier and enter the car park. Make a note of where you've parked!

🚪

On exit: Make your way to the exit barrier and insert the ticket you took on arrival. If you haven't overstayed, the barrier will automatically raise.

💳

Overstay? Any overstay fee is displayed on the exit column and can be paid by debit or credit card.

💡 Fun Facts

🛣️

From the M1, take J10 onto the A1081 and follow signs to London Luton Airport and the Long Stay Car Park. Postcode: LU2 9NW.

🚶

The Long Stay has a regular shuttle bus to the terminal — runs every 10–15 minutes and takes about 5 minutes.

📱

You can load directions straight to your phone from the booking confirmation email. No faffing with postcodes at 4am.

⏰

The Long Stay is the cheapest on-site option but factor in shuttle time — allow an extra 15 minutes from parking to terminal.
